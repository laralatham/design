Interior Design

Office To Employee Lounge

Brief: Reconfigure an unused office space in the ECE corporate office to an employee lounge that could also be used to meet with potential clients.

While working on a small budget, I created a fun and dyanmic space that was reflective of the brand's colors and the atmosphere of this entertainment company. I utilized their proprietary photos for the artwork, and added a cling logo to the window that could be viewed from the hallway. I painted the accent wall and installed this space with the help of my amazing husband.

Main Bedroom Flourish

Brief: Work with owners' existing furniture and layout to create a luxurious, relaxing sanctuary.

Created visual consistency throughout their house utlizing a paint color used elsewhere and added accent picture frame moulding on each wall, laid out in a way that would frame applicable pieces. Added long draping blackout curtains to frame their giant window, but kept all existing furniture.
